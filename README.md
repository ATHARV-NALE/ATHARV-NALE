## 🧑‍💻 About Me & My Work

I work across the entire robotics stack — from **microcontrollers & firmware (Embedded Systems)**, through **robotic middleware & control systems (ROS 2)**, up to **intelligent perception & planning (AI & LLMs)**.

> **My Mission:** To build robots that don't just follow scripts, but perceive, understand, and adapt to their environments in real time.

### 💎 Why Work With Me?

| | |
|---|---|
| 🦾 **End-to-End Capability** | Low-level register-level firmware in C for STM32, complex Python ROS 2 nodes, and edge-deployed AI/LLM inference — all under one roof. |
| 💰 **Cost-Efficient Development** | I build realistic **Gazebo simulations** to validate control loops, docking algorithms, and path planning *before* touching hardware — saving you prototyping cost and time. |
| 🧠 **Cutting-Edge AI Integration** | I specialize in fusing LLMs with ROS 2 for semantic, natural-language navigation and task reasoning — not just traditional scripted robotics. |
