## 🧑‍💻 About Me & My Work
I design and develop end-to-end intelligent physical systems. I work across the entire stack—from **microcontrollers & firmware (Embedded Systems)**, through **robotic middleware & control systems (ROS 2)**, up to **intelligent perception & planning (AI & LLMs)**.

My mission is to create robots that don't just follow scripts, but perceive, understand, and adapt to their environments in real time.

---

## 💎 Why Work With Me?
- **End-to-End Capability:** I can write low-level register configuration firmware in C for STM32, write complex Python ROS 2 nodes, and deploy AI neural nets/LLMs on the edge.
- **Cost-Efficient Development:** I build realistic **Gazebo simulations** to thoroughly test control loops, docking algorithms, and path planning *before* deploying to hardware, reducing physical prototyping costs.
- **Cutting-Edge AI Integration:** I don't just build traditional robots; I specialize in integrating LLMs with ROS 2 for semantic, natural language navigation and task reasoning.

---

## 🛠️ Freelance & Contract Services
- 📟 **Embedded Systems & IoT:** Custom firmware development (C/C++) for STM32, ESP8266, ESP32, and Arduino. RTOS, I2C, SPI, UART, and sensor integration (BME280, IMUs).
- 🤖 **ROS / ROS 2 Development:** Node development, subscriber/publisher setups, navigation stack tuning, custom actions, and FSM/Behavior Tree controllers.
- 👁️ **Computer Vision & AI:** ArUco tag detection, OpenCV-based color & object detection, pick-and-place manipulation algorithms, and LLM-assisted robotics.
- 🎮 **Simulation & Testing:** Setting up Gazebo and URDF models, virtual environments, sensor emulation, and simulation-based testing.
