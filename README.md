## 🧑‍💻 About Me & My Work

I work across the entire robotics stack—from **microcontrollers & firmware (Embedded Systems)**, through **robotic middleware & control systems (ROS 2)**, up to **intelligent perception & planning (AI & LLMs)**. 

> **My Mission:** To create robots that don't just follow scripts, but perceive, understand, and adapt to their environments in real time.

### 💎 Why Work With Me?
- 🦾 **End-to-End Capability:** I can write low-level register configuration firmware in C for STM32, write complex Python ROS 2 nodes, and deploy AI neural nets/LLMs on the edge.
- 💰 **Cost-Efficient Development:** I build realistic **Gazebo simulations** to thoroughly test control loops, docking algorithms, and path planning *before* deploying to hardware, reducing physical prototyping costs.
- 🧠 **Cutting-Edge AI Integration:** I don't just build traditional robots; I specialize in integrating LLMs with ROS 2 for semantic, natural language navigation and task reasoning.
